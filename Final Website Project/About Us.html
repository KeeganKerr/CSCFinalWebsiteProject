Why Make this website?

I Know that when I started out in blender doing everything through the long winding paths took up 80% of my time was spent manually selecting tools and searching for modifiers. Using keybinds cuts out 90% of that struggle for me now and even though its common knowledge not everyone knows these things. So for my website assignment I wanted to make a reference site for myself and others that I could easily add to. Anything I can do to make anyones lives easier earlier rather than later I'd like to contribute even if its only used by myself.
