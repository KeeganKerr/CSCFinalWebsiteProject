Welcome, to the world of Shortcuts

Hello and Thank you for deciding to use my website for your artistic needs. This websites main purpose is to show off some of the most useful and most time saving shortcuts you may not know about in the Blender Software!

There's not just shortcuts though, there are tips on modifiers and how each modifier group works and how they can be used to improve your workflow. All of which is important to make the artistic process as fun and engaging as possible.

Example of Modifiers

In case you don't know or you need a refresher modifiers are initially no permanent effects that you can apply to your objects in Blender. They range from altering the models themselves or controlling the way a model responds to physics or particles. They are incredibly useful and one of the few with a keybind is the Bevel modifier.

Examples of Tool shortcuts

Along with modifier shortcuts their are shortcuts for tools or actions, one of the most popular being the loop cut, CTRL+R. The loop cut is a tool that allows you to make an edge looping around the object. Another shortcut you might know is the object spawning shortcut Shift+A
